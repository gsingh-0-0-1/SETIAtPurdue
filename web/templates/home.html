Mission Statement

To question whether or not we are alone in the universe is one of the most profound, important questions in humanity's existence. Whether thousands of years ago gazing up at the heavens or in the modern day using some of the most advanced instruments ever built, that question can only be answered by all of us together.

And what lies beyond our thin, blue atmosphere is a variety of phenomena, more than even potential companions in the cosmos. From neutron stars to elusive fast radio bursts, the universe is riddled with the unknown.

We seek to make it possible for anyone, anywhere to look at data that we've collected and contribute to the search for everything interesting that lies above.
